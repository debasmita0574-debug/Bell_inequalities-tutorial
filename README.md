# Bell Inequalities Tutorial

This repository is a step-by-step tutorial on **Bell states** and **Bell inequalities (CHSH)**.

We explore the topic from **fundamental physics intuition** to **computational implementation using Qiskit**, helping learners build a deep and connected understanding.

---

## 📌 Topics Covered

- Bell States
- Bell Inequalities (CHSH)

---

## 🧠 What You Will Learn

1. Historical background of Bell states in quantum theory  
2. Intuition behind the Bell game  
3. Bell states and quantum entanglement  
4. CHSH inequality and its significance  
5. Qiskit-based simulation and experiments  

---

## 🚀 Motivation

Bell inequalities reveal that quantum systems exhibit correlations that cannot be explained by classical physics.  
This tutorial bridges the gap between **theoretical understanding** and **practical simulation**, making these concepts accessible and intuitive.

---

## 🔬 Key Insight

Quantum systems do not rely on pre-defined outcomes.  
Instead, they exhibit **strong correlations that emerge during measurement**, which leads to the violation of classical limits.
